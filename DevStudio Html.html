Developer Studio - Discord Rules

1. General Rules

  • All members must be at least 13 years old to join the server.
  • Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  • Each user is allowed to have only one account in the server. Multiple accounts are prohibited.
  • Users with 99 warnings will be permanently banned.

2. Text Channel Rules

2.1 Respectful Communication

  • No offensive language, personal attacks, or harassment.
  • Respect the opinions of others, even if you disagree.

2.2 Channel-Specific Usage

  • Use channels for their intended purposes (e.g., `#general` for casual chats).
  • Repeatedly posting off-topic content may result in warnings.

2.3 No Spamming or Advertising

  • Do not send spam messages or flood the chat.
  • External advertisements require admin approval.
  • Posting phishing links will result in an immediate ban.

3. Voice Channel Rules

3.1 Appropriate Behavior

  • No loud noises or disruptive behavior during voice chats.
  • Recording or streaming without permission is prohibited.

3.2 Minimize Background Noise

  • Use a proper headset and microphone to ensure clear communication.
  • Excessive background noise may result in removal from the channel.

4. Media and Content

  • No sharing of offensive or illegal content.
  • Memes and humor must be appropriate and posted in designated channels.

5. Administration

  • Admins reserve the right to enforce rules and resolve disputes fairly.
  • Users can report violations to admins with proper evidence.
